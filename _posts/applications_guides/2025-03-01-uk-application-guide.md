## Overview

The United Kingdom is home to world-renowned universities. This guide walks you through the application process for UK universities.

## Entry Requirements

- Secondary school certificates or equivalent qualifications
- IELTS or TOEFL scores (typically minimum 6.5 IELTS)
- Personal statement
- Academic references

## UCAS Application Process

Most UK universities use the Universities and Colleges Admissions Service (UCAS) system for undergraduate applications.

**Key Deadlines:**
- October 15: Early deadline for Oxford and Cambridge
- January 15: Main deadline for most universities
- June 30: Final deadline for all applications

**Application Steps:**
1. Create a UCAS account
2. Complete the application form
3. Submit with academic references and personal statement
4. Receive decisions from universities
5. Make your firm and insurance choices

## Visa Requirements

International students need a Student Visa (Subclass D of the Tier 4 system).

**Required documents:**
- Unconditional offer of admission
- Financial proof (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies - CAS statement)
- Valid passport
- English language certification
- Tuberculosis test (if applicable)

## Costs

- Tuition fees: £10,000 - £38,000 per year for international students
- Living expenses: £12,000 - £15,000 per year

## Timeline

| Timeline | Task |
|----------|------|
| 12 months before | Research and prepare |
| 9-10 months before | Submit UCAS applications |
| 3-6 months before | Receive conditional offers, meet conditions |
| 2-3 months before | Apply for student visa |
| 1 month before | Complete final arrangements |
