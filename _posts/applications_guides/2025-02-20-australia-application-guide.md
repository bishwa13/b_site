## Overview

Australia is a popular destination for international students. This guide covers the essential steps to apply to Australian universities.

## Entry Requirements

- Valid passport
- Academic transcripts
- English language proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL, or equivalent)
- Statement of purpose

## Application Timeline

### 12 Months Before
- Research universities and programs
- Gather required documents
- Prepare for language tests

### 9-10 Months Before
- Submit applications to universities
- Monitor application status

### 6-8 Months Before
- Receive conditional or unconditional offers
- Accept offer from your chosen university

### 3-6 Months Before
- Apply for student visa
- Arrange accommodation
- Make tuition fee payments

### 1-2 Months Before
- Complete final preparations
- Arrange travel

## Visa Process

The Australian student visa (subclass 500) is required for all international students. The process typically takes 4-6 weeks.

**Required documents:**
- Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE)
- Proof of financial capacity
- Health clearance (MIAC)
- Character certificate

## Costs

- Tuition fees: AUD $15,000 - $35,000 per year (varies by institution and program)
- Living expenses: AUD $20,000 - $30,000 per year
